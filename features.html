System capabilities

Run every step of service from a single control layer.

POS & KDS Suite

Tableside, counter, kiosk, and kitchen display modes with shared menus, modifiers, course control, and prep timers.

  • Mobile POS for iOS & Android
  • Kitchen routing by station
  • Combo and upsell prompts
  • Offline order capture

Inventory & recipes

Automated costing, supplier orders, batch tracking, tablet counts, and stock alerts to prevent waste.

  • Recipe version control
  • Supplier API connections
  • Auto purchase proposals
  • Expiry shelf-life alerts

BI & finance

Sales, margin, labor, and payment dashboards with exports to accounting, BI tools, and custom data warehouse feeds.

  • Multi-entity consolidation
  • Automated P&L snapshots
  • Labor productivity KPIs
  • Data warehouse streaming

CRM & loyalty

Segment guests, trigger personalised offers, manage gift cards, and launch subscription programs tied to POS.

  • Audience segments & tags
  • Automated offer builder
  • Stored value & gift card ledger
  • Marketing API & webhooks

Omnichannel hub

Sync menus with delivery platforms, ecommerce, order-at-table, and courier apps from one catalog.

  • Marketplace order ingestion
  • Centralised menu updates
  • Last-mile courier routing
  • Guest order tracking widgets

Security & compliance

Role-based permissions, device management, audit trails, fiscal integrations, and EU data residency.

  • Granular role policies
  • Audit & anomaly alerts
  • KassenSichV & TSE support
  • SAML & SCIM provisioning

End-to-end workflows

Vectis bridges every department. Connect POS events to kitchen, stock, finance, and customer engagement in real time.

Service operations

  • Table, counter, and kiosk ordering in one interface
  • Kitchen display with bump, hold, and recall actions
  • Automated tipping, splitting, and settlement workflows
  • Tablet checklists for opening and closing routines

Back office

  • Recipe cost updates automatically synced to POS
  • Suggested purchase orders with supplier confirmations
  • Batch and expiry logs with barcode or RFID support
  • Flash P&L and variance reports delivered daily

Engagement

  • Unified guest identities across POS, online, and loyalty
  • Triggered campaigns per guest milestone or behaviour
  • Feedback widgets linked to ticket or table numbers
  • Marketing performance dashboards with ROI tracking
